# Mini-app
ABC Mini-app is a prebuilt, hosted loyalty app optimized for customer engagement. It is a low-code integration that allows you to quickly create customizable customer engagement features to enable gamification on your website or application. 

![MiniApp](/miniapp.png)

## Getting started
The Mini-apps can be opened in two ways: **ABC Auth** and **JWT Auth**
To get started with the ABC Mini-App integration, you can follow these steps:
* Learn how the ABC Auth works
* Learn how the JWT Auth works
* Customize the Mini-App to suit your needs

Remember, the choice between ABC Auth and JWT Auth ultimately depends on your specific use case and requirements. Take the time to carefully evaluate your needs and choose the authentication system that best meets them.
